Welcome to Sip & Snacks Heaven, your ultimate destination for delightful beverages and delicious snacks! We're dedicated to providing you the very best of our offerings, with a focus on quality, customer service, and unique flavors.

Founded in 2024, Sip & Snacks Heaven has quickly become a beloved spot in the heart of Kathmandu. Our mission is to create a warm and welcoming environment where you can enjoy your favorite drinks and snacks.

We hope you enjoy our offerings as much as we enjoy making them for you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.

At Sip & Snacks Heaven, we offer a wide range of services to meet your needs:

  • Freshly brewed coffee and specialty teas.
  • Delicious pastries and baked goods.
  • Healthy and tasty sandwiches, salads and may more snacks items.
  • Cozy and comfortable seating for you to relax and enjoy.
  • Free Wi-Fi for all our customers.
  • Catering services for your special events.
